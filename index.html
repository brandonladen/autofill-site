Desktop (Chrome: dist folder)

Follow these exact steps to install the extension from the dist/ folder.

  1. Download the zip from the Downloads section on this page to your laptop and extract the dist folder.
  2. Make sure you have dist/ folder after extraction.
  3. Open Chrome and go to chrome://extensions.
  4. Enable Developer mode in the top right of the extensions page.
  5. Click Load unpacked and choose the downloaded dist/ folder.
  6. After the extension loads, open a quiz. The floating Autofill widget, a yellow lightbulb icon, appears only when you open a quiz page.
  7. To make the extension easy to find, pin its icon to the Chrome toolbar: click the puzzle icon in the toolbar, then click the pin next to the LaceFi Autofill entry.
  8. Look for this extension icon in Chrome and pin it to the toolbar:

How to use the widget

  1. Tap the yellow floating lightbulb to open the Autofill panel or open the extension popup from the toolbar.
  2. Enter your license key in the Enter License field or purchase credits in the popup as instructed.
  3. Use Autofill only for practice and study. See the Compliance section below.

Mobile (Quetta v1.7.5: ZIP file)

Follow these exact steps to install the extension using the extension ZIP on an Android device running Quetta v1.7.5.

  1. On your Android device, download Quetta Browser from the Google Play Store and open the browser if it is not already installed.
  2. Download the extension ZIP from the Downloads section on this page to your device.
  3. In Quetta, tap the three dots menu located at the bottom right of the screen and choose Extensions.
  4. Look for this extension icon in Quetta so you know what to show or tap:
  5. Select Manage Extensions
  6. In the manage extension window, click the red text Developer options
  7. click (from .zip/.crx/.user.js) shown in the Extensions screen.
  8. From the file picker, search for extension.zip and select the downloaded extension.zip in your device Downloads folder and confirm any permission prompts.
  9. After installation, ensure the extension is Enabled in Quetta's Extensions list.
  10. Open the three dots menu at the bottom right, choose Extensions, find LaceFi Autofill, and enable the option to show the extension icon in Quetta's browser UI so the icon appears on your screen.
  11. Open your Moodle site in Quetta, sign in, and open a practice quiz. The floating Autofill widget, a lightbulb icon, will appear on supported pages.

If the widget does not appear

  1. Open Quetta's Extensions list and verify LaceFi Autofill is enabled.
  2. Reload the Moodle quiz page in Quetta.(make sure you have opened a cat/ exam)

Licenses and payments

The system uses Paystack for secure payments and issues license keys. You can also receive manual license keys from your instructor or admin.

  • After purchase, paste the license into the extension popup or the floating widget's "Enter License" modal.
  • The extension rechecks the license with the backend before using credits.

Support & privacy

We only store license keys and minimal usage metadata on the server to track credits. Payment card data is handled by Paystack.
